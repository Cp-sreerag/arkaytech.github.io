About Arkay Technology

Arkay Technology Ltd. is an artificial intelligence company focused on building next-generation intelligent systems that can understand, reason, and assist humans across digital and physical environments.

Our work spans conversational AI, voice intelligence, computer vision, and predictive analytics. We design and develop autonomous AI platforms that enhance decision-making, automate workflows, and unlock new capabilities for individuals, enterprises, and governments.

Our long-term mission is to create evolving intelligent systems that integrate deeply into real-world applications, shaping the future of human-AI collaboration.

Technology

Our platforms are built using modern artificial intelligence and software engineering technologies including machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing, computer vision, and scalable backend systems.

Core stack includes Python, FastAPI, LLM systems, neural networks, and real-time inference infrastructure.
